About Me

A space to be human in your grief.

I know what it is to have your life split into before and after. GriefHacks is a quiet corner of the internet where you don’t have to perform, explain, or rush your way through loss.

Here you’ll find honest stories, practical tools, and gentle structure for rebuilding a life that can hold both love and sorrow. No toxic positivity. No fixing. Just real human experience, skillfully held.

If you’re looking for validation, language for what you’re feeling, or ways to steady yourself when the waves hit, you’re in the right place.
